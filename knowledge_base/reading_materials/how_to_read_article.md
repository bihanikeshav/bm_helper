## How to read an article

### What does "critically" reading an article mean? (printed in red in original)

The following twelve questions define what it means to read an article critically for this course:

i) What are the limitations of this reading?

ii) If I were to do this framework, how would I change it?

iii) Can you summarize the article in a line or two?

iv) New words you learnt in the article? Explain them in one line each.

v) What concepts in article agree/oppose concepts you know already?

vi) Has this concept already been taught under some other name?

vii) What are the most interesting things said in this article?

viii) What are the surprising omissions and typos?

ix) What are the most thought provoking things in the article?

x) What are the most irritating questions you can ask the author?

xi) Is this framework applicable to India? What will change?

xii) What domain specific changes will you make to the framework?
i.e. when you are applying this framework to telecom, pharma, face creams or lubricants or any other domain. How does that change the elements in the framework?
